Yacht Auction

NYPC CODE BATTLE · Online Round

Problem Description

After finishing their mushroom game, Yeti and Pink Bean have moved to Grandis. Reminiscing about the past when they threw dice using the Roll of the Dice skill, they play a board game called Yacht Auction.

Game Rules

  • A single match consists of 131313 rounds.
  • Each round consists of a Bidding phase and a Scoring phase.
    • Exceptionally, the first round skips Scoring phase, and the last, 131313th round skips a Bidding phase.

Bidding Phase

The bidding phase proceeds as follows:

  1. Two sets of dice, Set AAA (5 dice) and Set BBB (5 dice), are rolled. Each die is independent and identically distributed integer between 111 and 666 inclusive.
  2. Each player submits the dice set they wish to take (AAA or BBB) and a bid score between 000 and 100 000100\,000100000, inclusive.
  3. Each player take dice set based on following rules:
  • If the players choose different sets, each player takes their chosen set.
  • If both players choose the same set, the player who submitted the higher bid score takes the chosen set.
  • If both the chosen set and the bid score are identical, the winner is determined randomly. Every trial is independent and identical.
  1. Scores are then adjusted according to the following rules:
  • If a player successfully takes their desired set, they lose points equal to their bid score.
  • If a player fails to take their desired set, they gain points equal to their bid score.

Scoring Phase

From the 101010 dice currently in hand (exception: only 555 dice are available in the final scoring phase), the player selects 555 dice. Then, the player chooses one of the following Scoring Rules to earn points. Each scoring rule can be used at most once per match.

  • FIVE: Sum of dice showing 5×1 000\text{Sum of dice showing } 5 \times \mathbf{1\,000}Sum of dice showing 5×1000 points
  • SIX: Sum of dice showing 6×1 000\text{Sum of dice showing } 6 \times \mathbf{1\,000}Sum of dice showing 6×1000 points

Additionally, if a player earns a total of 63 00063\,00063000 points or more from the Basic Scoring Rules, they receive a Bonus Score of 35 000\mathbf{35\,000}35000 points.

Combination Scoring Rules

  • CHOICE: The total number of pips in five dice×1,000\textbf{The total number of pips in five dice} \times \mathbf{1,000}The total number of pips in five dice×1,000 points
  • FOUR_OF_A_KIND: If there are 444 or more dice of same value, The total number of pips in five dice×1,000\textbf{The total number of pips in five dice} \times \mathbf{1,000}The total number of pips in five dice×1,000 points. Otherwise, 0\mathbf{0}0 points.
  • FULL_HOUSE: If there are 333 dice of same value plus other 222 dice of same value, The total number of pips in five dice×1,000\textbf{The total number of pips in five dice} \times \mathbf{1,000}The total number of pips in five dice×1,000 points. Otherwise, 0\mathbf{0}0 points.
  • SMALL_STRAIGHT: If there are 444 dice which form a consecutive sequence (1234,2345, or 34561234, 2345, \text{ or } 34561234,2345, or 3456), 15,000\mathbf{15,000}15,000 points. Otherwise, 0\mathbf{0}0 points.

  • YACHT: If all 555 dice have the same value, 50,000\mathbf{50,000}50,000 points. Otherwise, 0\mathbf{0}0 points.

Final Score and Victory Condition

After all rounds are completed, the player with the higher total score wins. If the total scores are equal, the game ends with draw.

  • Player’s Total Score=Points from Basic Rules+Points from Combination Rules+Bidding Points\text{Player's Total Score} = \text{Points from Basic Rules} + \text{Points from Combination Rules} + \text{Bidding Points}Player’s Total Score=Points from Basic Rules+Points from Combination Rules+Bidding Points
